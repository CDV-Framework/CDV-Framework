# CDV-Framework
Co-creation of Democratic Value: Framework for evaluating digital public participation platforms

## What is this?

CDV evaluates how digital platforms distribute power in participatory processes.

**7 Dimensions (each scored 1-5):**
- Co-Design - Who designs the process?
- Co-Agenda - Who sets topics?
- Co-Production & Co-Governance - Who creates content and manages the platform?
- Co-Analysis - Who interprets data?
- Co-Evaluation - Who assesses impact?
- Inclusion - Are marginalized groups included?
- Integrity - Is it transparent, accountable, sustainable?
